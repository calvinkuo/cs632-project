Need help?
  • 🎨 Brush: Default tool for drawing.
  • 🧼 Eraser: Use to erase lines (toggle off to return to brush).
  • 🔄 Undo/Redo: Step backward or forward through your drawing actions.
  • 🗑️ Clear: Clears everything (you can undo it too now!).
